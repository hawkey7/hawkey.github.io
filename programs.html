Incubator Programs

Annually, there are three batches of programs. Ethereum Classic Labs will provide startups with funding, office space, training, workshops, networking and other resources. Help will be provided to all applicants with creating a business plan, market research, analysis, preparation of investment pitch and searching for suitable investors.

What makes the program special is that we provide you with a list of potential application topics on ETC Blockchain and a hands-on guide to go through business process as long as you know the blockchain technology and coding! Within 3-month, startups will receive training from both technology and business aspects as well as innovative shared office space where gathering all the talent people all around the world. We invest between $50,000 to $150,000 based on companies valuation, in exchange for 9% equity.

What We Offer

Fund

$50,000 - $150,000 as initial funding for Research & Development.

Space

Located in either downtown San Francisco or Singapore. Open plan office design with private meeting rooms available.

Network

Ethereum Classic Labs will help applicants to cultivate relationships and partnerships with investors, industry leaders, successful entrepreneurs and leading consulting and legal firms.

Idea

If applicants have a great idea but require additional input on its application or other business support, our industry experts will guide the applicant on the best application of their product in fields such as Finance, Betting & IoT.

Business Support

We provide more than just a prototype of a business plan. Ethereum Classic Labs strives to ensure the startups we invest in are more competitive in the marketplace. We provide workshops in marketing strategy, user-friendly product design, accounting, pitch training and one-to-one mentor sessions to help optimize the business.

Technology

A series of blockchain workshops will be held, the majority of which will focus on the ETC blockchain and smart contract platform. Applicants will have the opportunity to collaborate with ETCDEV team, IOHK, Ethereum Commonwealth, and other experienced developers such as Igor Artamonov, Isaac Ardis and Charles Hoskinson.
