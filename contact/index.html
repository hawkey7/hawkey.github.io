—— FAQ

  • Do you invest in the companies that go through the Program?

    We invest in all companies that take part in the Program. Companies receive an investment of $50,000 - $150,000 for 9% of their equity.

  • When shall I apply?

    Ethereum Classic Labs have a rolling admission and invite startups to submit applications throughout the year.

  • How will the program be select?

    We will read every application. Promising founders and interesting idea are the bonus points in selection.

  • What will I get if I have been admitted to the program?

    Successful applicants are provided with office space within our modern, open plan working space in either downtown San Francisco or Singapore for a period of three months. This gives startups the opportunity to work collaboratively with selected blockchain industry leaders and help shape the business model under expert guidance. At the end of each program, a demo day will be held which is attended by our network of investors which can help to secure another round of funding for the applicants.

  • I’ve applied and haven’t heard back. When will I get an answer?

    We receive thousands of applications and we will read each application. We will try to respond to applicant but it will take several weeks to finish the process.

  • Can I apply again if I was rejected previously?

    Yes, of course. It is often the case that most successful founders got rejection on their first try. Anytime when you keep making progress and have a new idea, you are always encouraged to re-apply to our program.
